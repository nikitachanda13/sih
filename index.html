About Floatchat

Revolutionizing Ocean Data Discovery

Floatchat is an innovative AI-powered platform designed specifically for oceanographic data analysis. Our mission is to make complex ARGO float data accessible and actionable through advanced AI and intuitive visualization tools.

By integrating ultra-fast Groq LLM technology, we enable researchers, scientists, and ocean enthusiasts to interact with ocean data in natural language, receiving instant insights and visual representations of complex ocean phenomena.

Our platform transforms the way we understand and interact with ocean data, making it easier to discover patterns, track changes, and make informed decisions based on comprehensive ocean monitoring.
