Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize access to complex oceanographic data. We believe that by providing an intuitive, natural language interface for the ARGO dataset, we can empower a new generation of scientists, students, and policymakers to make data-driven discoveries about our oceans.

The Technology

FloatChat is built on a foundation of cutting-edge AI and data visualization technologies. We leverage large language models to understand user queries and translate them into actionable data requests, presenting the results in beautiful, interactive dashboards.
